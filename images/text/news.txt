Saudade Association
We founded the Saudade Association 6 years ago. The goal of the association is to raise funds for the purchase of equipment needed for the children's departments of the General Hospital in Dubrovnik. That's why every year, on the last Saturday in November, we organize the '24 hours without compromise' campaign.

Udrugu Saudade osnovali smo prije 6 godina.
Cilj udruge je skupljanje sredstava za kupovinu uredjaja potrebnih dječjim odjelima Opće bolnice Duborvnik.
Zbog toga svake godine, zadnju subotu u studenom, organiziramo “24 sata bez kompromisa” akciju.
