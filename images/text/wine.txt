We have divided the wine list, as well as the menu, into the regions of Croatia and our native Herzegovina. We have made an effort to find the best winemakers and hidden gems in order to present you with a truly unique wine story.

The effort, hard work, sweat, rewards, tastes, and aromas that the vineyard brings into one bottle with the help of winemakers, and the secrets of the wine that will delight your palate, we will convey to you with great pride.


Vinsku kartu, kao i jelovnik, podijelili smo na regije Hrvatske i naše rodne Hercegovine.
Potrudili smo se pronaći najbolje vinare i skrivene dragulje, kako bismo Vam prezentirali uistinu jedinstvenu vinsku priču.

Trud, rad, znoj, nagrade, okusi i mirisi sve što vinograd donese u jednu bocu uz pomoć vinara, a tajne vina koje okusi vaše nepce prenijeti ćemo Vam s puno ponosa.
