ImoCka cake	
A cake made from almond and two tablespoons of fine flour, with zest and juice from citrus fruits - oranges and lemons. Without baking powder.
The cake, as well as its preparation method, is under the UNESCO protection as intangible heritage.

ImoCka torta
Torta spravljena od bademovog i dvije žlice glatkog brašna, kora i sok od citrusa - naranče i limuna. Bez praška za pecivo. Torta, odnosno način njezine izrade nalazi se pod UNESCO zaštitom nematerijalne baštine.

Chocolate cake with arancini
Chocolate cake, orange marmalade, arancini - candied orange peel.

Čokolada i ´arancini` 
Čokoladni kolač, marmelada od naranče, arancini - ušećerena kora od naranče.

Cheesecake with honey and truffles
We make the cream for our cake from fresh cheese, sweet cream, lime, and almond liqueur. We finish it with honey and freshly grated truffles. 

Kolač od sira s medom i tartufima
Kremu za naš kolač radimo od svježeg sira, slatkog vrhnja, limete i likera od badema. Završimo s medom i naribanim svježim tartufima.


Ruddy apple cake
We've added this cake to our menu because it reminds us of all the Sunday feasts we've had with our family. A simple apple cake that evokes emotions.

Jabuka rumena
Ovaj kolač smo stavili na jelovnik jer nas podsjeća na sve nedjeljne objede koje smo proveli s našom obitelji. Jednostavan kolač od jabuke koji budi emocije.


Figs and olives
An unusual dessert. Dried figs in pasta, shaped like ravioli. White chocolate cream and homemade forest fruit sauce.

Smokva i maslina (sezonski)
Jedan neobičan desert. Suhe smokve u tjestenini, oblik raviola. Krema od bijele čokolade i domaćeg umaka od šumskog voća.
