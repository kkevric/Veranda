We serve memories

When you arrive at the restaurant, you will be greeted with wide smiles from our waitstaff and our bar, which we are particularly proud of. Our family, our ancestors, our past will let you know how much creating memories and passing on stories about our grandparents means to us.

From the very beginning, our mission has been to create memories with the guests who come to our doorstep. The slogan by which we are known, 'we serve memories,' came naturally and accurately reflects our desire to cook food that will stay in your memory for a long time, just as their recipes and dishes bring us back to our childhood.
